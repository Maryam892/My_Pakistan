Noor Mahal

×

Noor Mahal is a great palace which was built by the fifth ruler of Bahawalpur State, Nawab Sir Muhammad Sadiq or also known as Shahjehan of Bahawalpur, who was famous for his passion of constructing beautiful buildings.

Darbar Mahal

×

Darbar Mahal is a palace in the city of Bahawalpur, Pakistan. The building was built to hold courtly events and government offices of the former princely state of Bahawalpur. The palace was built by Bahawal Khan V, and initially named Mubarak Mahal.

Gulzar Mahal

×

Gulzar Mahal is a palace in the city of Bahawalpur, Pakistan, that was built between 1906 and 1909. It was commissioned during the reign of Sadeq Mohammad Khan V, and was built to be the residence for women members of the royal household of the former princely state of Bahawalpur, aside from the Queen.

Jamia Mosque, Al-Sadiq

×

Al-Sadiq Mosque is a mosque located in Bahawalpur, Punjab, Pakistan. Its foundation stone was laid by Great Sufi of Chishtia clan and the Spiritual Master of Nawab of Bahawalpur Noor Muhammad Maharvi more than 200 years ago.

SS World Park

×

SS World Amusement Park has been opened in the city of Bahawalpur, it is a state of the art amusement park which provides entertainment and enjoyment for the whole family. SS World Amusement Park offers indoor and outdoor games, water stream, waterfall and also has one of the best food courts in the city of Bahawalpur.

Central Library

×

The second largest library of Punjab, the Bahawalpur Central Library is famous for its treasury of books. It contains a whole array of subjects and thousands of documents including rare manuscripts of historical importance
